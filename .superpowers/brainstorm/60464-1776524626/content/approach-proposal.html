What gets cut or demoted

CUT
  • Scatter plot — visually busy, doesn't support "systematic" thesis
  • Drift histogram — methodology detail, not narrative
  • Severity flip matrix — becomes a stat callout instead
  • CWE bar chart as separate page — folds into "Who Disagrees"
  • Lightning bolt emoji — replaced by clean typography
KEEP
  • Conflicts by Year — hero chart on homepage, shows growth
  • Directional Bias line chart — proves "systematic" thesis
  • Vector breakdown metric cards — compact, informative
  • CNA bar chart — names names, compelling
  • CWE bar chart — shows which vuln types are worst
