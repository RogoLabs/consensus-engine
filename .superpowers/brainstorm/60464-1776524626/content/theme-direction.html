A

Go Light

Matches rogolabs.net, cve.icu, cnascorecard.org. Feels cohesive as a portfolio. Clean, professional, editorial. Better for embedding/sharing screenshots in articles and presentations (light backgrounds print better).

B

Stay Dark

Feels more "security tool" — the audience expects dark themes. Charts pop more against dark backgrounds. Differentiates from your other sites (this is a research tool, not a portfolio piece). Matches CVE.ICU's dark mode exactly.

C

Light default + dark toggle

Like cve.icu — light default for the editorial feel and printability, with a dark mode toggle for practitioners who prefer it. More work to implement but serves both audiences.
